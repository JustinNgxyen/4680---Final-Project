Type Conversion-------------------------------------------

    Code:
age = "20"
ten_years_later = 10 + age
print(f"In ten years you'll be {ten_years_later}")

    Error:
    TypeError: unsupported operand type(s) for +: 'int' and 'str'





Indexes---------------------------------------------------

    Code:
numbers = [1, 2, 3]
print(numbers[3])

    Error:
    IndexError: list index out of range





Reserved keyword------------------------------------------

    Code:
items_deleted = 50
del = 10
items_deleted += del

    Error:
    SyntaxError: Invalid syntax
